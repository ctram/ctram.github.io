FosterPals

I live with two amazing dogs, both rescued from animal shelters. There is a myth that shelter dogs are somehow not on par with dogs from breeders, which is just not true. FosterPals was created with this in mind and seeks to connect animal rescue groups with gracious people who wish to foster animals until they find their forever homes!

Built with: Rails, Backbone.js, jQuery, Bootstrap, PostgreSQL

Space Debris! is my take on the classic game from the golden age of gaming, Asteroids. Piloting your trusty spaceship, you are responsible for ridding the space ways of hazardous debris! Be careful not to accidentally collide with the debris you are trying to dispose of!
